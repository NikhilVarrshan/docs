---
title: "15.2 Definitions"
---

**1. High-Impact Operational Days**

High-impact operational days include:

•	Working days immediately before or after weekends

•	Working days immediately before or after statutory holidays

•	Any other days formally notified as critical delivery or operational windows

**2. Minimum Operational Quorum**

Minimum Operational Quorum refers to the minimum number of team members and critical roles that must be available on a working day to ensure safe and effective operations.
