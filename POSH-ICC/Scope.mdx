---
title: "Scope and Applicability"
---

**This policy applies to:**

- All full-time, part-time, probationary, and contractual employees
- Consultants, advisors, directors, and officers
- Interns, trainees, apprentices (with appropriate adaptations)
- Vendors, clients, visitors, and third parties interacting with the workplace

**It applies to conduct occurring:**

- At the workplace
- During work-related travel
- At offsite meetings, events, conferences, or social gatherings
- Through digital platforms, messaging apps, email, or social media
- Outside the physical workplace if there is a workplace nexus
