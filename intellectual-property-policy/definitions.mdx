---
title: "12.2 Definitions"
---

For the purposes of this policy:

**3.1 Intellectual Property (IP)**

“Intellectual Property” includes, but is not limited to:

- software code, source code, scripts, libraries, APIs,
- system architecture, frameworks, workflows, pipelines,
- algorithms, models, prompts, datasets, training methods,
- technical documentation, internal playbooks, SOPs,
- product designs, prototypes, wireframes, UX flows,
- business plans, pricing strategies, financial models,
- research, analyses, reports, presentations,
- inventions, discoveries, improvements, ideas, concepts,
- trade secrets, confidential know-how,
- derivative works and modifications of the above.

**3.2 Work Product**

“Work Product” means any output, deliverable, or contribution created, authored, designed, developed, or contributed to by an employee, whether individually or jointly with others.

**3.3 Portfolio Companies**

“Portfolio Companies” include any startup, venture, or entity in which the Company has invested, incubated, advised, built, or otherwise supported.
