---
title: "12.4 Confidentiality And Use Restrictions"
---

Employees must not, during or after employment:

- disclose non-public or proprietary information,
- share internal methodologies, architectures, or strategies,
- reuse Company or portfolio IP for personal or third-party benefit,
- assist competitors or third parties using Company-derived knowledge.

These obligations apply regardless of whether the information is explicitly marked as confidential.
