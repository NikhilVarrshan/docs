---
title: "12.5 Publications, Blogs, and Technical Content"
---

### **1. General Restriction**

Employees must not, without prior written approval from the Company:

- publish blogs, articles, or posts based on Company or portfolio work,
- publish technical deep dives derived from internal systems or processes,
- disclose architectural, strategic, or operational insights,
- publish case studies or retrospectives of Company or portfolio projects.

This includes:

- personal blogs,
- Medium, Substack, Dev to, GitHub write-ups,
- conference slides,
- whitepapers or tutorials,
- open-source releases based on internal work.

### **2. Generic Content Exception (Limited)**

Generic content may be permitted only if it:

- does not reference Company or portfolio specifics,
- does not reveal proprietary methods or workflows,
- could reasonably be created without access to Company work,
- is reviewed and approved where required.

Approval is discretionary and may be refused without obligation to justify.
