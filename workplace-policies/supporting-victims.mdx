---
title: "2.4 Supporting Victims"
---

Zemuria is committed to supporting individuals affected by harassment or violence.

Support may include, based on circumstances:

- Coverage of relevant medical expenses
- Access to mental health or counseling support
- Legal assistance or guidance where appropriate
- Adjustments to work arrangements during recovery

All discussions and support measures will be handled with discretion and confidentiality.

Early reporting is encouraged to prevent escalation and ensure timely intervention.
