title: "2.3 Workplace Violence"
---

**Workplace Violence**

Violence or threats of violence are considered severe misconduct.

This includes:

- Physical assault
- Sexual violence
- Threats or intimidation
- Destruction of property
- Severe verbal abuse

**If you witness or suspect violence:**

1. Report immediately to HR / Manager
2. Contact building security or emergency services if required
3. Prioritize your safety - do not intervene physically
4. Confirmed acts of violence will result in immediate termination and may involve law enforcement.
