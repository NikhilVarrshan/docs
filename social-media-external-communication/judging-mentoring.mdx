title: "13.7 Judging, Mentoring & Advisory Roles"
---

### **8. Permitted Activities**

**Employees may participate as:**

- judges at hackathons,
- mentors at accelerators,
- panel reviewers or advisors,

**provided that:**

- there is no conflict of interest,
- Company or portfolio confidential information is not disclosed,
- the role does not imply Company sponsorship or endorsement without approval.

**8.1 Conflict & Attribution Restrictions**

**Employees must not:**

- disclose internal evaluation criteria or decision-making processes,
- use Company or portfolio insights to influence external outcomes improperly,
- represent themselves as acting on behalf of the Company unless authorized.
