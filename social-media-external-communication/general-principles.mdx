title: "13.2 General Principles"
---

### 3. Employees must ensure that external communications:

- are accurate and factual,
- do not disclose confidential or non-public information,
- do not misrepresent the employee’s role or authority,
- do not create confusion regarding Company or portfolio ownership,
- do not harm the Company’s reputation or relationships.

Unless expressly authorized, employees do not speak on behalf of the Company or its portfolio companies.
