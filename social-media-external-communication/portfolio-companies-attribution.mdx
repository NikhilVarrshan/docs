title: "13.8 Portfolio Companies & Attribution"
---

### **9. Prohibited Claims**

**Employees must not:**

- claim to have built, founded, or owned portfolio companies,
- represent portfolio products or outcomes as personal work,
- disclose portfolio strategies, roadmaps, or internal discussions,
- imply personal authority over portfolio ventures.

**This applies across:**

- resumes and CVs,
- LinkedIn profiles,
- websites and portfolios,
- interviews, talks, and social media.

### 9.1 Permitted Descriptions

**Employees may describe involvement factually, such as:**

- “contributed as part of a venture studio team supporting portfolio companies,”
- “worked on engineering tasks under Company direction.”
- Descriptions must not exaggerate responsibility or imply ownership.
