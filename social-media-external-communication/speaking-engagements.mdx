### **7. Permitted Participation (With Conditions)**

Employees may participate in external events such as:

- conferences,
- meetups,
- podcasts,
- webinars,
- panels,

**only if:**

- content is limited to general, non-confidential topics,
- Company or portfolio work is not disclosed or implied,
- prior approval is obtained where required.

**7.1 Topics That Require Approval**

Prior written approval is required before speaking on:

- Company technologies, processes, or strategies,
- portfolio company experiences or lessons,
- venture studio methodologies,
- internal tooling, playbooks, or frameworks,
- case studies derived from Company or portfolio work.

**7.2 Prohibited Representations**

Employees must not:

- present themselves as founders or decision-makers of portfolio companies,
- imply ownership or leadership authority over Company ventures,
- speak as an official Company spokesperson unless authorized.
