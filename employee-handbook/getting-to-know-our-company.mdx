title: "1.2 Getting to Know our Company"
---

Zemuria is an American Venture Studio that builds and scales product-driven businesses. We create ventures in-house and partner with founders through structured programs, including our Founders-in-Residence track, to take ideas from early validation to real execution.

We began with e-commerce and learned firsthand what it takes to win in competitive markets: clear positioning, tight execution, and uncompromising standards. That experience shaped how we operate today - fast cycles, honest feedback, and decisions grounded in real-world signals.

Zemuria works with a global network of founders and operators. Many of our product owners and portfolio CEOs come from major innovation hubs including the San Francisco Bay Area and New York City, bringing experience, ambition, and market proximity. Our operational headquarters is based in Pondichéry (Puducherry), India, where our core execution teams run day-to-day operations across product, engineering, and support functions.

As a venture studio, we allocate time and capital with discipline. We back initiatives that demonstrate traction and clarity, and we are equally willing to pause, reshape, or stop efforts that do not align with market reality. This protects focus, raises the bar, and ensures we build things that can endure.

A defining characteristic of Zemuria is how closely we work with founders and internal teams. We treat iteration as normal: course corrections are expected, pivots happen, and learning is continuous - so long as decisions are made responsibly and performance stays accountable.

### What this means for you

Working at Zemuria is not “just a job.” You will be trusted with meaningful responsibility early. Priorities may evolve as ventures evolve. We value people who are adaptable, execution-oriented, and comfortable working in an environment that rewards ownership and high standards.

You’ll work on real priorities from Day 01. You’ll be expected to take ownership, communicate clearly, and adapt as ventures change. If you like fast execution, direct feedback, and high accountability, you’ll fit in here.
