---
title: "10.5 References and Employment Verification"
---

HR will issue official employment verification and experience letters.

References may be provided only to employees who leave the Company in good standing.

Managers may provide personal references at their discretion, provided they are factual and non-misleading.

The Company is not obligated to provide references.
