---
title: "10.6 Notice Period Buyout"
---

Notice period buyout is not an entitlement.

Approval of a buyout request is entirely at the discretion of the Company, exercised by HR.

The Company may, at its discretion:

- approve or refuse a buyout request,
- require partial service of the notice period,
- require monetary compensation in lieu of notice.

The Company is not obligated to approve a buyout request or provide reasons for refusal.
