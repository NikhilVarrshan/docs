**7. Company-Issued Equipment**

**7.1 Ownership**

All company-issued devices, equipment, and furniture remain the property of the Company unless otherwise stated in writing.

**7.2 Usage & Care**

Employees are responsible for reasonable care and proper use.

Equipment must not be sold, transferred, or lent to others.

**7.3 Repairs & Damage**

Accidental damage must be reported immediately.

Damage caused by negligence, misuse, or violation of policy may result in recovery of repair costs.

**7.4 Data & Access**

Employees should avoid storing sensitive personal data on Company devices.

The Company may access or retrieve devices or data for security, compliance, investigation, or exit purposes, in accordance with law.
