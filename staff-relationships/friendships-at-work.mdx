---
title: "6.6 Friendships at Work"
---

Friendships among colleagues are a natural part of the workplace and are permitted.

### **Employees must ensure that friendships do not result in:**

- favoritism or exclusion,
- sharing of confidential information,
- gossip or inappropriate discussions,
- interference with professional judgment or productivity.
