---
title: "6.9 Workplace Visitors"
---

**Employees hosting visitors are responsible for ensuring that:**

- prior approval is obtained where required,
- visitors sign in, present identification, and wear visitor badges,
- visitors are escorted at all times in work areas,
- visitors do not access confidential information,
- photography, recording, or device access is restricted unless authorized.
- Special care must be taken when visitors are minors.
