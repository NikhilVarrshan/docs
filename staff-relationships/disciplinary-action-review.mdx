---
title: "Disciplinary Action & Policy Review"
---

Violation of this policy may result in disciplinary action, proportionate to the nature and severity of the conduct, up to and including termination of employment, subject to applicable law and Company process.

Disciplinary action will consider:

- failure to disclose conflicts,
- abuse of authority,
- breach of confidentiality,
- harassment or retaliation,
- disruption of the workplace.

**Policy Review:**

The Company reserves the right to amend this policy to reflect changes in law, business structure, or operational needs.
