---
title: "6.8 Employment of relatives"
---

The Company follows a merit-based approach and does not prohibit employment of relatives.

However:

1. No employee may supervise or report to a relative.
2. Employees must not participate in hiring, evaluation, promotion, compensation, discipline, or termination decisions involving a relative.
3. Relatives include immediate and extended family members, domestic or live-in partners, and equivalent close relationships.
4. Any change in relationship status after employment must be disclosed promptly.
