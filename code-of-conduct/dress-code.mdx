---

### 1. Purpose

Zemuria operates as a strictly professional, secular, and identity-neutral workplace.

Our objective is to ensure that all employees, clients, founders, partners, and visitors experience an environment defined solely by work, merit, professionalism, and mutual respect - free from religious, political, caste-based, tribal, or ideological influence.

This policy applies uniformly to all employees, contractors, interns, trainees, apprentices, visitors, and representatives of Zemuria.

### **2. Workplace Neutrality Principle**

Zemuria is a secular workplace.

This means:

- The company does not endorse, practice, promote, display, or facilitate any religion, belief system, political ideology, caste, tribe, or identity group.
- The workplace is not a venue for personal belief expression beyond private thought.
- No belief system - majority or minority - is given visibility, preference, or accommodation within company premises or official spaces.

This neutrality is foundational to ensuring equality, inclusion, and freedom from social pressure or symbolic dominance.

### **3. Dress Code (Professional & Neutral)**

**General Standard**

Zemuria follows a Business / Business Casual / Smart Casual dress code depending on role and context.

**All attire must be:**

- Clean
- Professional
- Non-distracting
- Neutral in appearance
- Appropriate for a shared professional environment

### **4. Explicit Prohibitions**

The following are not permitted inside company premises, during work hours, or in any official Zemuria context (including virtual meetings):

- Religious attire, symbols, markings, or accessories (including but not limited to visible religious threads, beads, markings, headgear, insignia, or ceremonial clothing)
- Caste, tribal, or community-identifying symbols or attire
- Political symbols, slogans, attire, badges, or colors
- Attire or accessories associated with ideological movements, sects, or groups
- Any visual representation that signals religious, caste, tribal, or political identity to others

This applies regardless of personal belief, tradition, or cultural background.

### **5. Client-Facing & External Representation (Strict Standard)**

When engaging with:

- Clients
- Investors
- Founders
- Vendors
- External partners
- Public events representing Zemuria

The dress code is strictly professional. No religious, political, caste, or ideological identifiers - of any form - are permitted. Employees are expected to represent only Zemuria.

### **6. Religious Practices & Activities**

Inside the workplace Zemuria does not conduct, host, organize, or permit:

- Religious ceremonies
- Prayers
- Rituals
- Pujas
- Chanting
- Worship activities
- Religious observances of any kind

No dedicated or informal spaces for prayer, worship, or religious practice are provided or permitted. No religious gatherings - formal or informal - may occur within company premises.

**6.1 Personal Beliefs**

Personal beliefs are respected as private matters.

- Employees may observe their beliefs outside work hours and outside company premises.
- Employees may take leave for religious observance as per the Leave & Holiday Policy.
- Religion remains personal and private, not organizational.

### **7. Symbols, Installations & Visual Displays**

The following are strictly prohibited in all company spaces, including desks, walls, meeting rooms, digital workspaces, and internal chat platforms:

- Religious images, idols, photos, posters, icons, or texts
- Political symbols or portraits
- Caste or tribal symbols
- Ideological or belief-based imagery

**This includes:**

- Physical spaces
- Virtual backgrounds
- Profile photos used in official tools
- Shared digital workspaces
- Internal communication channels

Zemuria spaces must remain symbol-neutral.

### **8. Company Celebrations & Events**

**Company events are:**

- Cultural
- Social
- Non-religious
- Non-divisive

**Examples include:**

- Team meals
- Social gatherings
- Cultural festivities without religious rituals
- Non-religious symbolic activities (e.g., food, decor, games)

**Zemuria does not perform or include:**

- Religious rituals
- Worship activities
- Ceremonial prayers
- Religious symbolism

### **9. Prohibited Conduct**

**The following behaviors are strictly prohibited:**

- Proselytizing or attempting to influence othersâ€™ beliefs
- Religious, political, caste-based, or ideological campaigning
- Coercive or persuasive discussions related to belief systems
- Displaying symbols that may create discomfort, exclusion, or perceived dominance
- Introducing tribalism, group identity signaling, or ideological alignment into the workplace

### **10. Enforcement & Consequences**

**Violations of this policy will be treated as Code of Conduct breaches and may result in:**

- Formal warnings
- Disciplinary action
- Removal of prohibited materials
- Suspension
- Termination of employment or contract

Repeated or deliberate violations will result in stricter consequences.

### **11. Interpretation & Authority**

This policy is intentionally comprehensive and blanket.

- No exceptions, accommodations, or carve-outs are implied unless explicitly stated in writing by the company.
- Interpretation and enforcement authority rests with HR.

In case of ambiguity, the interpretation that best preserves workplace neutrality will prevail.This policy is not anti-religion. It is pro-professionalism, pro-equality, and pro-neutrality.

Zemuria is a workplace - not a religious, political, or ideological space
